]]> http://localhost/wordpress/assembled-fisher-delta/feed/ 0 Printing a Pipboy 3000 VI on Fisher http://localhost/wordpress/printing-a-pipboy-3000-vi-on-fisher/ http://localhost/wordpress/printing-a-pipboy-3000-vi-on-fisher/#comments Wed, 11 Nov 2015 14:59:25 +0000 https://reprappro.com/?p=17651 Due to the recent release of Fallout 4 we decided to try and make the iconic Pip-Boy 3000 Mark VI on our 3D printers using Yvo de Haas’ design available at ytec3d.com

The pictures are of our 108% model printed almost entirely on Fisher delta 3D printers, except for the screen surrounds that were printed on a Mendel as they were just too large to fit on the Fisher.

All the parts were printed at 0.15mm layer height with support for overhangs generated automatically by Slic3r. The parts were then de-brimmed and de-burred with a sharp knife and finally assembled.
